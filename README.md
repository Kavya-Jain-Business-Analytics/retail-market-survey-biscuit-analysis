# Retail Market Survey and Sales Analysis of Biscuit Brands

## Project Overview

This project involved conducting a primary market survey of retail stores in New Market, Bhopal, to understand biscuit purchasing patterns, retailer preferences, consumer demand, and market trends.

Data was collected through direct interaction with shopkeepers and retailers. The collected data was cleaned, organized, analyzed, and visualized using Microsoft Excel.

## Objectives

* Identify dominant biscuit brands in the local market
* Analyze consumer buying preferences
* Understand retailer stocking patterns
* Evaluate the impact of promotional activities
* Generate actionable business insights through dashboard reporting

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Dashboard Design
* Data Cleaning

## Key Findings

### Brand Performance

* Parle-G emerged as the leading biscuit brand in the ₹5 segment.
* Parle-G and Good Day were the strongest competitors in the ₹10 segment.

### Consumer Behaviour

* Families represented the largest customer segment.
* Taste was identified as the primary factor influencing purchase decisions.

### Market Trends

* Consumer preference is gradually shifting toward ₹10 biscuit packs.
* Discount offers had the highest impact on sales performance.

### Retail Insights

* Average daily sales ranged between 3 and 7 units across surveyed stores.
* Retailers reported stronger demand for established brands with high customer trust.

## Outcome

The project demonstrated the application of market research techniques, data analysis, and dashboard development to generate business insights from real-world retail data.
